About

The Engineer
Behind The Sound

At 21, Colin Ishe Makoni Jr. is one of the youngest engineers operating at the highest tier of live production on the African continent — and the pace of his career makes every year count double.

His foundation was built at Events Evolution, Zimbabwe's premier live production company, under mentors Tatenda Rushwaya, Blessing Bero, and Endelani Kachepa. That environment forged instinct, not just technique.

Specialising in monitor engineering, Colin creates the perfect on-stage sonic world for every artist — translating stage chaos into a controlled, personal environment that lets performers do what they do best.

For most artists he has worked with, his role was as their personal production technician: managing backline, liaising on technical requirements, ensuring everything was dialled before the first note. Work that took him to Botswana, Zambia, and beyond.

"Remember, when doing monitors — it's all about what the artist wants."

— Martyaudio · Colin's mentor & hero in audio
